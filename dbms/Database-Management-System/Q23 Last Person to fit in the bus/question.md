## 23: Last Person to Fit in the Bus

**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Tags:** sql, database, sorting

**Problem Reference:**

* Platform: LeetCode  
* Link: https://leetcode.com/problems/last-person-to-fit-in-the-bus/description/

**Concept to Read:**

* SQL `ORDER BY` and `LIMIT`

**Issue Description:**

Find the **last person** who can fit on the bus given the capacity limits and the list of people in queue based on their arrival.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

* Order people by their arrival in the queue  
* Return only the person who is the last to fit based on bus capacity

**Bonus:**

* Use sorting and limiting techniques (`ORDER BY`, `LIMIT`)
